# Research Notes - Agent C

## C-001 Curantis Solutions

- Sector fit: healthcare technology
- Evidence basis: AWS explicitly labels Curantis an SMB in Texas and the company website is public.
- Problem signal: fragmented clinical data, FHIR-related interoperability needs, and the need for faster analytics access across the business.
- Sources:
  - https://aws.amazon.com/smart-business/case-studies/healthcare-smb-transforms-data-analytics-with-aws-healthlake/
  - https://curantissolutions.com/
- Risk note: among the strongest healthcare candidates.

## C-002 Digital Practice

- Sector fit: healthcare services
- Evidence basis: described as a therapy practice with workflow-automation needs; SME signal is reasonable but not yet strong.
- Problem signal: repetitive backend administration was consuming time better spent on the practice itself.
- Source: https://www.zoho.com/flow/customers/digital-practice.html
- Risk note: useful lead but needs stronger size evidence and official website.

## C-003 HealthServe

- Sector fit: health and social services
- Evidence basis: Zoho Desk lists 11-50 employees and the organization has a public website.
- Problem signal: intake and follow-up for migrant-worker medical and counseling needs required more structured service management.
- Sources:
  - https://www.zoho.com/desk/customers/healthserve.html
  - https://www.healthserve.org.sg/
- Risk note: strong service-operations candidate.
