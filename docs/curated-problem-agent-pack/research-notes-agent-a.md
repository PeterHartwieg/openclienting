# Research Notes - Agent A

## A-001 TIE

- Sector fit: manufacturing
- Evidence basis: Odoo case says TIE is a sportswear manufacturer in Egypt and used 20 Odoo users.
- Problem signal: outdated ERP, poor integration capability, and costly vendor dependency.
- Source: https://www.odoo.com/blog/customer-reviews-6/odoos-transformation-elevating-sportswear-manufacturing-at-tie-1448
- Risk note: needs official website and non-vendor corroboration before curation.

## A-002 Mobilia Industry Company

- Sector fit: manufacturing
- Evidence basis: Odoo case presents Mobilia Factory as a furniture manufacturing facility with 5 users.
- Problem signal: disconnected software failed to provide clarity and efficiency.
- Source: https://www.odoo.com/blog/customer-reviews-6/smart-manufacturing-made-simple-mobilia-factorys-success-with-odoo-1525
- Risk note: strong operations problem, but company website still needs confirmation.

## A-003 CTS Industries

- Sector fit: manufacturing
- Evidence basis: Odoo categorizes the customer as a small organization with 10-49 employees.

- Source: https://www.odoo.com/blog/customer-reviews-6/enhancing-efficiency-and-visibility-cts-industries-transformation-1715

## A-004 Lakeside CNC Group

- Sector fit: manufacturing-adjacent repair and maintenance
- Evidence basis: Zoho Desk case says the company started as a two-person repair shop and scaled beyond spreadsheets; official FANUC control repair website confirms specialist SME operation in Bensenville Illinois.
- Problem signal: spreadsheet-based request handling and weak repair-management visibility could not keep pace with growth.
- Sources:
  - https://www.zoho.com/desk/customers/lakesidecncgroup.html
  - https://fanuccontrolrepair.com/
- Phase 1 decision: advance.
